◆ Order flow (the hidden edge)

Data says WINS have LOWER buy-share (0.37) than losses. A low number = sellers dumping = capitulation = everyone who wanted out is out. (SHORT mirrors: requires buying to dominate the high.)
Capitulation prints on heavy volume. 1.0 = average, 5.0 = 5× spike.
Wins average $894K of dollar volume; losses average $2.7M — a 3× gap, the strongest signal in the data. Thin coins snap back violently; heavyweights absorb the flush and keep bleeding.
